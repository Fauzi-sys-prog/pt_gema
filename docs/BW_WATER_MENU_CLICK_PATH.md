# BW Water Menu Click Path

Panduan ini dibuat supaya demo bisa dijalankan hanya dengan mengikuti urutan menu dan kata kunci pencarian.

## 1. Commercial & Sales

### Data Collection

- buka menu `Commercial & Sales`
- masuk ke `Data Collection`
- search: `BW Water`
- klik `Lihat Detail`
- opsional:
  - klik `Preview`
  - klik `Word`
  - klik `Excel`

### Quotation

- masih di `Commercial & Sales`
- buka `Quotation`
- search: `001/BWW/GMT/II/2026`
- buka detail quotation
- opsional export `Word` atau `Excel`

## 2. Project

### Project List

- buka menu `Project`
- search: `PRJ-BWW-2602-001`
- buka detail project jika perlu

## 3. Supply Chain Hub

### Purchase Order

- buka menu `Supply Chain Hub`
- masuk ke `Purchase Order`
- search: `PO/BWW/II/2026/001`

### Receiving

- masih di `Supply Chain Hub`
- masuk ke `Receiving`
- search: `RCV/BWW/III/2026/001`

### Vendor Expense

- masuk ke `Vendor Expense` atau halaman expense vendor
- search: `EXP/BWW/III/2026/001`

## 4. Monitoring Gudang / Inventory

### Inventory Center

- buka menu `Inventory`
- masuk ke `Monitoring Gudang` atau `Inventory Center`
- search: `MAT-CS-001`
- tunjukkan:
  - stok item
  - tombol `Registrasi SKU`
  - tombol `Input Manual Inbound`

### Material Request

- masih di menu inventory / supply chain yang relevan
- buka `Material Request`
- search: `MR/BWW/III/2026/001`

### Stock In

- buka `Stock In`
- search: `SI/BWW/III/2026/001`

### Stock Out

- buka `Stock Out`
- search: `SO/BWW/III/2026/001`

## 5. Production

### SPK

- buka menu `Production`
- masuk ke `SPK`
- search: `SPK/BWW/III/2026/001`

### Work Order

- masih di `Production`
- buka `Work Order`
- search: `WO/BWW/III/2026/001`

### Production Report

- buka `Production Report`
- search: `PRD-BWW-2602-001`

### Production Tracker

- buka `Production Tracker`
- search: `PTR-BWW-2602-001`

### QC Inspection

- buka `QC Inspection`
- search: `QC-BWW-2602-001`

## 6. Logistics / Correspondence

### Surat Jalan

- buka menu `Surat Menyurat` atau `Logistics`
- masuk ke `Surat Jalan`
- search: `SJ/BWW/III/2026/001`

### Proof of Delivery

- buka `Proof of Delivery`
- search: `POD-BWW-2602-001`

### Berita Acara

- buka `Berita Acara`
- search: `BA/BWW/III/2026/001`

## 7. Finance & Ledger

### Customer Invoice / Piutang

- buka menu `Finance & Ledger`
- masuk ke `Piutang` atau `Customer Invoice`
- search: `INV/BWW/III/2026/001`
- tunjukkan status: `Paid`
- opsional export `Word` atau `Excel`

### Accounts Payable

- masih di `Finance & Ledger`
- buka `Accounts Payable`
- search vendor: `PT Global Steel`
- atau search expense/invoice yang terkait `BW Water`

### General Ledger

- buka `General Ledger`
- tampilkan export kalau dibutuhkan

### Bank Reconciliation

- buka `Bank Reconciliation`
- tampilkan export kalau dibutuhkan
