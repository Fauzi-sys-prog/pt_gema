## Urutan Klik

1. `Data Collection`
   - cari `BW Water`
   - klik `Lihat Detail`
   - klik `Preview` atau `Word`

2. `Quotation`
   - cari `001/BWW/GMT/II/2026`
   - buka / export

3. `Project`
   - cari `PRJ-BWW-2602-001`

4. `Purchase Order`
   - cari `PO/BWW/II/2026/001`

5. `Receiving`
   - cari `RCV/BWW/III/2026/001`

6. `Monitoring Gudang`
   - cari `MAT-CS-001`
   - tunjukkan `Registrasi SKU`
   - tunjukkan `Input Manual Inbound`

7. `Material Request`
   - cari `MR/BWW/III/2026/001`

8. `SPK`
   - cari `SPK/BWW/III/2026/001`

9. `Work Order`
   - cari `WO/BWW/III/2026/001`

10. `QC Inspection`
   - cari `QC-BWW-2602-001`

11. `Surat Jalan`
   - cari `SJ/BWW/III/2026/001`

12. `Berita Acara`
   - cari `BA/BWW/III/2026/001`

13. `Piutang / Customer Invoice`
   - cari `INV/BWW/III/2026/001`
   - tunjukkan `Paid`
   - export

## Narasi Pendek

`Flow ini dimulai dari data collection BW Water, lalu turun ke quotation, project, supply chain, gudang, produksi, logistics, dan finance. Jadi satu data survey bisa mengalir sampai invoice dan pembayaran tanpa putus antar modul.`
